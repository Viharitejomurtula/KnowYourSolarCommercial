What We Do

Not a recommendation.
A foundation for your decision.

Property managers face mounting pressure around solar. KnowYourSolar gives you the honest, technical picture — so you walk into every conversation fully prepared.

01
Roof & Site Analysis
We assess usable roof area, orientation, tilt, obstructions, and structural considerations using satellite imagery and address-level data — no site visit required.
02
Production Modeling
Annual output is estimated through NREL's PVWatts engine — the same tool used by engineers and utilities — delivering credible, traceable projections.
03
Financial Feasibility
Payback period, savings range, and incentive eligibility are presented conservatively. We never inflate numbers. All uncertainty is disclosed explicitly.
04
Structured Report
A documented deliverable you can share with ownership groups, boards, or prospective installers — a professional artifact, not a sales deck.
